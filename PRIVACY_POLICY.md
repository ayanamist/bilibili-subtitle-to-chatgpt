# Privacy Policy

## Bilibili Subtitle to ChatGPT

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to any third-party server operated by the developer.

The extension only interacts with Bilibili (to read subtitle data) and ChatGPT (to send subtitle text), both of which are initiated by the user.

If you have any questions, please open an issue at https://github.com/ayanamist/bilibili-subtitle-to-chatgpt/issues.
